My Favorite Recipe: Kaldereta!

Kaldereta is one of my favorite Filipino dishes because it's flavorful with a hint of spice, and it's usually shared during family gatherings.

Ingredients

[I don't know the specific amounts lol]

  • Beef
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Bell Peppers
  • Liver spread
  • Onion and garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Saut√© garlic and onion.
  2. Add beef and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in broth and simmer until tender.
  4. Add tomato sauce and liver spread, stir well.
  5. Put potatoes and carrots, cook until soft.
  6. Add bell peppers, season, and simmer briefly.
  7. Serve hot with rice.
